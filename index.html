Summary: Survival Status by Demographics

In April 1912, the infamous RMS Titanic sank during it's first voyage across the North Atlantic Ocean. The purpose of this analysis is to explore the survival status from the shipwreak in comparison to the demographics of the 2,477 passengers and crew. Of that total, 2,208 have a known survival status, and thus serves as the maximum available persons we can explore.

The categories included are: Person Type, Boarded City, Age, Gender, and Nationality. For each demographic categories, there are some individuals with unknowns, but each demographic dataset were seperated out from the full dataset to ensure we could compare the highest available person count. Use this site to follow the journey of Miss Marison Wright: She was a 26 year old English woman who boarded the Titanic in Southampton, England. Use this information to hypothesis if she was "Saved" or tragically "Lost". Find the answer and a sample of her ticket on the "PersonType" tab, so check there last!
